This is a Thai version of the Jackson font. Jackson was shown in 1971 by dry-transfer manufacturer Mecanorma and credited to B. Jacquet [Mecanorma 1988]. Several sources give the full name as Bernard Jacquet [Reichardt/Hoefer]. I first saw Jackson on a tailor shop in Bangkok. It also included a custom made version of the sign in Thai. Since there was no Thai version of Jackson, I decided to create one.
